Frequently Asked Questions

Application

How long is the application process?

- It depends on how quickly you can complete all the procedures from evaluation to requirement submission and interview process. Usually, it takes 5-10 working days to receive the hiring decision; however, this process can take longer because the team picks candidates thoroughly.

Can I re-submit my application?

- You have three months from the date of your interview to reapply.

Do I need to go to your office to apply?

- No, personal appearance is not required. The whole application is online.

Can I apply for a specific educator?

- Absolutely yes, you can enroll in a specific Educator you want. The Availability Page will provide you with all registered Educators on the website.

What are the steps in applying as an educator?

- Create an account with ______ and fill out an online application form.

- Complete a short demo lesson. Our teams will review it, and if you show you have the necessary skills, you'll hear from us!

- Slay the interview process and wait for the confirmation email.

- Complete your profile and sign your contract. You're ready to start teaching!

Requirements

What are the requirements to apply as Educator?

- A valid diploma or proof of graduation, NBI clearance, and a government-issued ID.

- Should have strong language skills (both oral and written).

- Internet speed of at least 10 Mbps or higher.

- Have proper camera, headset, and microphone.

- Will be working at home but should be in a quiet and well-lit space environment.

What is the requirement for students to register?

- Create an account with ______ and fill out an online application form.

- Submit the requirements like School Name and School ID.

- Complete your profile, and you're ready to start learning!

Do I need to have any teaching experience to qualify?

- It is encouraged but not required if you have prior teaching experience. Passion and a strong work ethic are far more critical than teaching experience.

Teaching

Do you accept part-timers?

- Yes, we welcome part-time employees. Your schedule will be determined by the information you will provide in your profile.

Can I cancel Teaching Session?

- We accept cancellations up to 48 hours before the scheduled session via your Educator Dashboard. Only medical emergencies supported by a medical certificate are permitted cancellations less than 24 hours before the session.

For the students

How long is my application/registration to be verified?

- Your application/registration verification can be as fast as 1-3 days.

Is it possible for me to set my schedule?

- You can choose the Educator's schedule you are compatible with.

Where can I find the courses, I enrolled in?

- You can find your enrolled courses by clicking your "learners tab," which will show you the enrolled subjects you have.

Can I un-enroll courses?

- Yes, if the reason is valid/acceptable by the terms and conditions of the website.
